The Wonders of Ancient Egypt

Exploring the history and marvels of one of the world's oldest civilizations.

Pyramids of Giza

The pyramids are monumental tombs constructed for Egypt's pharaohs and remain as one of the most enduring symbols of ancient engineering and culture.

The Great Sphinx

Carved out of limestone, the Great Sphinx is an iconic statue that has fascinated historians and travelers for centuries.

Egyptian Hieroglyphics

Hieroglyphics, the formal writing system of ancient Egypt, used a combination of logographic and alphabetic elements to record their language.
